## Strategic Principles

These are the rules we make decisions by.

| # | Principle |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Consistency beats size** — $120/month every month beats one $1,000 deposit once |
| 2 | **Automate everything possible** — human decisions fail, systems don't |
| 3 | **Never gut the portfolio for one move** — max 20–30% of portfolio value deployed at once |
| 4 | **Leverage is seasoning, not the meal** — borrow conservatively and late, not early |
| 5 | **Don't let money in without rules** — governance before expansion |
| 6 | **Document before you need it** — write the rules while everyone agrees |
| 7 | **Separate the businesses from the fund** — clean books, clean splits |
| 8 | **Trust but verify government assumptions** — never plan around assistance without confirming the rules |
| 9 | **The goal is the mission, not the numbers** — generational continuity, not short-term gains |
| 10 | **Build rivers, not a bucket** — multiple automatic inflows, one growing lake |
