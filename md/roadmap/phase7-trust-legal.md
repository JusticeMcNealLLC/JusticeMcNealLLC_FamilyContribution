---

## Why a Trust?

The current structure is **Justice McNeal LLC** — owned by individual members. The long-term goal is to have the LLC owned by a **Family Trust**, which:

- Keeps assets out of probate (no court process on death)
- Protects assets from creditors and lawsuits
- Enables smooth generational handoff (defined succession)
- Can hold the LLC, real estate, vehicles, and policies all under one entity
- Can span generations — grandchildren, great-grandchildren

See `md/wealth/foundation.md` for the full strategic breakdown.

## Key Decisions

| Decision | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Revocable vs. Irrevocable | Revocable = flexible, easy to change; Irrevocable = stronger asset protection but harder to undo |
| Trustee | Likely Justin as initial trustee; name 2–3 successor trustees |
| Beneficiaries | All current LLC members + their direct descendants |
| Distribution rules | Define how assets are distributed (age requirements, conditions, etc.) |
| LLC operating agreement | Must be amended to reflect Trust as member/owner |
