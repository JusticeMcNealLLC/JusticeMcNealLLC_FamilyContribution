# Phase 10 — Family Compound / Real Estate

**Status:** 🔲 Not Started (Long-Term Vision)  
**Priority:** Low  
**Milestone unlock:** 🔒 $500,000 (Compound Vision tier)  
**Goal:** Family-owned land with affordable housing for members.

---

## The Vision

The LLC/Trust purchases land. On it: a house, duplex, multi-unit, or multiple small homes (container homes are a strong candidate — see `md/wealth/property.md`). Members can live there at **below-market rent**. Rent covers property maintenance, taxes, insurance, and contributes back to the LLC fund. The family builds equity collectively.

## Member Rent Structure

| Member Status | Rent |
|---------------|------|
| Active member, on-time contributor | Below-market (e.g., 60–70% of market) |
| Lapsed member | Market rate or eviction per agreement |
| Non-member family | Market rate |

> Rent income → LLC operating fund → partially re-invested
