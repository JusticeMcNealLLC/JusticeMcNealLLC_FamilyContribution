# 💰 Justice McNeal LLC — Contribution System

## Overview

The **Contribution System** is the financial foundation of the Justice McNeal Generational Wealth Plan.

Every member contributes capital into the family investment pool through recurring monthly payments and optional additional deposits.

These contributions fund:

• Long-term investments  
• The family lending pool  
• Life insurance premiums  
• Future family benefits  

Through consistent contributions, the family collectively builds an investment portfolio designed to grow for generations.

---

# Core Principles

The Contribution System is designed around five key principles:

### 1. Consistency
Small contributions made consistently over time compound into significant wealth.

### 2. Accessibility
Membership is designed to be accessible to family members regardless of income level.

### 3. Growth
All contributions are invested in long-term assets to maximize compounding.

### 4. Transparency
Members can see their contributions and the overall trust growth through the portal.

### 5. Generational Impact
Each contribution helps strengthen the financial future of the entire family.

---

# Minimum Contribution

To maintain active membership, a member must contribute at least:


$30 per month


Members may contribute more at any time.

Higher contributions accelerate both:

• Personal borrowing capacity  
• Overall trust growth

# Contribution Types

The system supports two types of contributions.

---

## 1. Monthly Contributions (Subscription)

Members subscribe to a recurring monthly contribution through Stripe.

Examples:

| Plan | Monthly Contribution |
|-----|----------------------|
| Basic | $30 |
| Standard | $50 |
| Builder | $100 |
| Custom | Any amount |

Members can upgrade their contribution level at any time.

---

## 2. One-Time Contributions

Members may make additional deposits outside of their monthly subscription.

Examples:

• bonus contributions  
• tax refund deposits  
• gifts into the family fund  
• extra investing during market dips

One-time deposits accelerate personal credit score growth and increase borrowing limits.

---

# Contribution Tracking

Each member's financial activity is tracked within the system.

Tracked metrics include:

• Lifetime contributions  
• Monthly contribution streak  
• Last contribution date  
• Total deposits (subscription + one-time)  

These values influence the **Member Credit System**.

---

# Contribution Streaks

Members who maintain consistent contributions build a **Contribution Streak**.

Example:


12 months of uninterrupted contributions


Benefits of long streaks:

• Increased credit score  
• Higher lending trust  
• Recognition in the system  

Contribution streaks reset if a member cancels or misses payments.

---

# Missed Contributions

If a member misses a monthly payment:

• Contribution streak may reset  
• Credit score may decrease  
• Borrowing privileges may be reduced  

Members may restore their status by resuming contributions.

---

# Contribution Adjustments

Options include:

• Increasing monthly contribution  
• Decreasing monthly contribution  
• Making one-time deposits  

Members are encouraged to increase contributions as their financial situation improves.
