# 🏛️ Justice McNeal LLC — Member Benefits

The Generational Wealth Plan provides members with financial benefits designed to build long-term stability and opportunity for the entire family.

Membership is built around **contribution, participation, and shared growth**.

---

# Core Benefit: Collective Investment

All member contributions are pooled together and invested in long-term assets.

Current portfolio allocation:

- **VTI** — Vanguard Total Stock Market ETF
- **VXUS** — Vanguard Total International Stock ETF
- **VIG** — Vanguard Dividend Appreciation ETF
- **SPAXX** — Fidelity Government Money Market Fund (liquidity)

These investments form the foundation of the family wealth system.

Members benefit from:

- Long-term market growth
- Dividend income
- Collective capital strength

---

# Benefit: Family Lending Program

The Generational Wealth Plan allows members to borrow from the trust.

Instead of relying on banks with high interest rates, members can access **low-interest family loans**.

## Lending Pool

Up to **30% of the total trust value** may be allocated for loans.

Liquidity is primarily held in **SPAXX**.

---

## Borrow Limits

Members may borrow between **15% and 30% of their total contributions**, depending on their **member credit tier**.

| Tier | Borrow Limit |
|-----|--------------|
| Bronze | 15% |
| Silver | 20% |
| Gold | 25% |
| Platinum | 30% |

Example:

If a member contributed **$10,000**:

Borrowing range:

$1,500 – $3,000 depending on credit tier.

---

# Benefit: Member Credit System

Members earn credit points through positive participation.

Points determine borrowing limits and other privileges.

Members gain credit through:

- Consistent monthly contributions
- Long membership history
- Responsible loan repayment
- Participation in the family system

Higher credit tiers unlock greater financial flexibility.

---

# Benefit: Life Insurance Program

Eligible members may receive life insurance coverage funded by the LLC/Trust.

## Policy Structure

For insured members:

- Policy owner: Justice McNeal LLC / Trust
- Beneficiary: Justice McNeal LLC / Trust
- Insured: Member

The trust pays policy premiums.

---

## Eligibility

Participation requires:

- Medical underwriting approval
- Premium costs within acceptable limits

Members who do not qualify can still participate fully in the Generational Wealth Plan.

---

# Death Benefit Structure

When an insured member passes away:

The life insurance payout is received by the trust.

Distribution:

- **30% allocated to the Descendant Pool**
- **70% allocated to the Main Investment Pool**

---

# Descendant Accounts

Funds allocated to descendants remain inside the trust.

Instead of direct inheritance payouts, funds are placed into **descendant borrowing accounts**.

This ensures the inheritance continues to grow.

Descendants may access funds through:

- Education loans
- Business funding
- Personal financial support

All funds remain invested while unused.
