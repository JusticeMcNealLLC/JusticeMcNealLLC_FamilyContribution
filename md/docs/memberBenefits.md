At 7% average annual returns, $30/month over 30 years is $38,000+ — and that's your slice before the fund's full compounding effect from everyone contributing together.

### 2. Low-Interest Family Loans
Once the fund is large enough, members can **borrow from the family fund** instead of a bank.

| Comparison | Bank | Family Fund |
|------------|------|-------------|
| Interest Rate | 10–25% | 4–6% |
| Who gets the interest | The bank | Returns back to our fund |
| Speed | Weeks + hard credit pulls | Internal — fast |
| Terms | Credit score & income based | Based on participation tier |

Up to 30% of total fund value is set aside as the lending pool. Your CP (Credit Point) tier inside the system determines your borrowing limit and interest rate. The more consistently you participate, the better your terms.

### 3. Birthday Payouts — Already Running Today
Every member who links their bank account gets **$10 automatically sent to their bank on their birthday.** This runs on a daily cron job. It's live and working right now. Small, but it shows the system does what it says it does.

The portal has a gamification layer — quests that earn Credit Points, status tiers (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Diamond), and badges that display on your profile. It keeps the fund engaging and ties participation to real perks like better loan terms.

### 6. A Social Layer — It's the Family's Digital Home
Social feed, family profiles, family tree, events system, and more. Members interact, post updates, celebrate milestones, and stay connected — all in one place that belongs to us.

### 7. Life Insurance (Planned)

This requires significant capital to get there — which is exactly why every dollar and every new member matters now.
