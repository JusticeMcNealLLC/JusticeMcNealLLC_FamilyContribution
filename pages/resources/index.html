Member Rules & FAQ

The rules, no fine print. These apply to every member — today, and anyone who joins later.

Rule What It Means
$30/month minimum Non-negotiable — this keeps the fund funded.
No wage or income requirements We will never ask for a percentage of your paycheck or raise. Ever.
Your contributions are yours Permanently tracked and recorded under your name. Not pooled anonymously.
Voluntary above minimum Contribute more whenever you want. It's always welcome, never required.
Windfalls are a culture Got a tax refund? Drop some in if you want. No one tracks it or expects it.
Same rules for everyone Cousins, aunts, extended family — when they join later, the bar is exactly the same.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pause my contributions?

Yes — contact Justin. Temporary pauses are handled case by case.

What happens if I leave?

Your contribution history is preserved. Withdrawal policy is still being formalized and will be documented clearly before rollout.

Who can see my information?

Your payment history is visible to you. The overall fund balance is visible to all members. Admins have limited oversight access for operational purposes.

 

Is this legal?

Yes. Justice McNeal LLC is a legally formed LLC. Contributions are treated as member investment. The Trust structure (Phase 7) will add an additional legal protection layer.

How do I get paid out?

Birthday payouts are automatic via Stripe. The lending program and major payouts are milestone-gated — they unlock as the fund grows.

How do I join?

Ask Justin for an invite. Setup takes less than 10 minutes from your phone.
