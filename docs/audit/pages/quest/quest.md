# Quest & Credit System

## 🧠 Overview
This system assigns **Credit Points** to members based on actions and behavior.

Credit Points directly impact:
- Borrowing eligibility
- Interest rates
- Access to perks & benefits

---

## 🔐 Credit Types

### 1. Permanent Credit (Lifetime)
- Earned once and **never removed**
- Represents trust built through setup and milestones

### 2. Dynamic Credit (Behavior-Based)
- Earned through ongoing activity
- **Can increase OR decrease**
- Loss of eligibility (missed payments, broken streaks) will reduce points

## 💳 Contribution Behavior Quests (Dynamic Credit)

### On-Time Payment
**Type:** Dynamic (Monthly)  
**Requirement:**  
- Make payment before due date  

**Rewards:**  
- Credit Points: +10  
- Cosmetic: 1 Month Streak Banner

**Penalty (if missed):**  
- No reward  
- May impact streak quests  

---

### 3-Month Contribution Streak
**Type:** Dynamic (Streak-Based)  
**Requirement:**  
- 3 consecutive on-time payments  

**Rewards:**  
- Credit Points: +50 (active while streak is maintained)
- Cosmetic: 3 Month Streak Banner
- Cosmetic: 3 Month Streak Badge

**Reset Rule:**  
- Missing a payment resets streak to 0  
- **All streak points are removed**

---

## ⚠️ Credit Behavior Rules

### Streak Logic
- Streak quests are **active bonuses**, not permanent rewards  
- Losing a streak = losing all associated points  
- Restart begins from 0  

---

### Credit Score Philosophy
- **Consistency > One-time actions**
- Members with steady contributions will have higher credit power
- System is designed to mimic **real-world financial trust**
